Document Tracker

Track the status of every document. Some take weeks — start early.

⏳ Important timing note: Do NOT apply for your police clearance, health certificate, proof of 12 years secondary education or bank statement yet. These documents have expiry dates and must only be obtained AFTER the company requests them and provides their specific format requirements. Applying too early means they may expire before you need them.
✓ Prepare now: Your passport, degree certificate, university transcript, passport photographs, CV and cover letter. These have no expiry issues and should be ready before you apply.
