Money Saving Tips
Name a savings account "Japan Fund" — seeing the name keeps you motivated every time you transfer.
Use Wise or Remitly for international transfers — fees are much lower than traditional banks.
Cooking at home in Japan saves approximately ¥20,000 to ¥30,000 per month compared to eating out regularly.
